PluckNote Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

PluckNote ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information PluckNote collects and how we use it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

PluckNote does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of the following:

  • The content of your notes
  • Your personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts
  • Your usage analytics
  • Your behavior patterns

Data Stored in Your Personal iCloud

Your notes, including text, colors, and metadata, are stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. PluckNote has no access to this data. All data is end-to-end encrypted between your devices through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure.

How We Use Data

PluckNote does not use your data for any purpose other than providing the app's core functionality (creating, editing, syncing, and deleting notes) directly on your devices. There is no server-side processing of your data by us.

Premium Purchase

When you purchase PluckNote Premium, Apple processes the transaction. We receive only the transaction confirmation and the anonymized product identifier ("com.plucknote.premium"). We do not receive your Apple ID, payment information, or billing address.

Analytics

PluckNote does not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track your behavior, page views, or feature usage.

Children

PluckNote does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not target children and contains no content inappropriate for any age group.

International Users

PluckNote is available worldwide. Your data is stored in the region associated with your iCloud account (managed by Apple). We make no claim to international data transfers because we do not transfer your data anywhere.

Your Rights

  • You can delete any note at any time within the app
  • You can delete your data by uninstalling the app (iCloud copy persists for 30 days per Apple's retention policy)
  • You can request information about your data by contacting us at the address below (we will respond with confirmation that we hold no data)

Third-Party Services

PluckNote uses only Apple's first-party frameworks:

  • CloudKit (for sync)
  • StoreKit (for Premium purchase)
  • WidgetKit (for Home Screen widgets)
  • SwiftUI / UIKit (for user interface)

We do not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will notify you through an in-app message and update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will require your continued use of the app to indicate acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests:

  • Email: chixiang1983@gmail.com
  • Response time: within 7 business days

Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China for users in China, and the laws of your jurisdiction elsewhere. Any disputes will be resolved through friendly negotiation first.
