Every save sounds like a plucked string

The "pluck" is synthesized locally via a Karplus-Strong physical model — no audio assets, no network calls. In Settings you can even use your notes to "play" a melody of Jasmine Flower.

We don't sell your privacy.

PluckNote collects no note content, no personal data, and no third-party SDK telemetry. Your data belongs to you — and only you.

1

No server

Notes, colors and dates live in your personal iCloud. The developer has no way to read them.
